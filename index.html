Exploring Items by Image

In this section you can explore the available items in my website. List of the items Names , Prices and Image links are stored in separate arrays . The idea of this section came from the Image carousel project. By clicking on the Next and Previous buttons you can navigate through the items in the corresponding array. The item’s name and price will be shown below the image. By clicking on Add to the shopping cart , they will be listed in the section bellow (Shopping Cart).

Shopping Cart

This section lists every item in your shopping cart . Every time you select an item, a new list item will be created, and the name and price will be shown. The total price will be shown in the end. In the beginning, an initial message says that the shopping basket is empty . By adding the first item, this initial message will be removed and replaced by the shopping basket list. Also, a new button will appear as Continue to checkout for payment. However, by pressing it, a text will appear that says this option will be added in the future (according to the project's guidelines). You can test all these by refreshing the page and adding new items.

    Commenting

    In this section you can write your Comments . Every time you enter a comment, it will be shown as a new list item . The name input is optional and if it is empty, it will be replaced by the word “Unknown”. If the comment input is empty, it is not valid and nothing will happen (“if” CYF blocks are used here). To submit the comment, you should click on the Submit button. The total number of the comments will be updated every time a comment is submitted. In the beginning, an initial message says that there is no comment . By submitting the first comment, this initial message will be deleted and replaced by the first comment list.
